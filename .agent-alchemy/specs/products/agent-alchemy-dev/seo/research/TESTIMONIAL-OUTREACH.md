## Testimonial Collection Guidelines

### What Makes a Great Testimonial?

**Good (Generic):**
> "Agent Alchemy is great! It saved us a lot of time on specifications."

**Better (Specific):**
> "Agent Alchemy's Research Agent analyzed 12 competitors in 47 minutes. That used to take our team 3 days. We shipped the GitHub App feature 2 weeks faster."

**Best (Specific + Emotional + Metric):**
> "I was drowning in spec work — 80 hours per feature. Agent Alchemy cut that to 10.6 hours. For the first time in 5 years, I'm not working weekends to hit sprint deadlines. Our CTO approved 3 more hires because we proved the ROI."

### Key Elements to Capture

1. **Before State (Problem):**
   - What was broken?
   - How much time/money was wasted?
   - What was the emotional cost?

2. **After State (Solution):**
   - What changed?
   - Specific metrics (hours saved, features shipped, cost reduced)
   - How do they feel now?

3. **Proof Point:**
   - Real numbers (10,287 lines, 70 hours saved, $10K cost reduction)
   - Specific feature or use case
   - Timeline (from 3 weeks to 2.6 hours)

### Questions to Ask in Follow-Up

1. "What was the biggest surprise after using Agent Alchemy?"
2. "If a friend asked 'Should I try this?', what would you say?"
3. "What specific result could you measure? (hours, cost, features, etc.)"
4. "Who on your team got the most value? Why?"
5. "What would you tell your past self before trying this?"
