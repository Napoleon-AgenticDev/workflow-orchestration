## IX. Content Style & Voice Guidelines

### A. Tone Principles

**Do:**
- ✅ Be direct and specific ("73x faster" not "much faster")
- ✅ Use active voice ("AI generates specs" not "specs are generated")
- ✅ Address pain points viscerally ("PR comments that make you want to quit")
- ✅ Show real numbers ($216K, 47 minutes, 10,287 lines)
- ✅ Use "you" language (second person, conversational)

**Don't:**
- ❌ Use corporate speak ("leverage synergies")
- ❌ Be vague ("improve productivity" → "save 70 hours per feature")
- ❌ Oversell without proof ("revolutionary" → show the data)
- ❌ Use passive voice excessively
- ❌ Bury the lede (lead with strongest point)

### B. Headline Formula

**Problem-Solution-Proof:**
> **[Pain Point]** + **[Solution Benefit]** + **[Proof Point]**

**Examples:**
- ✅ "Stop Reviewing AI Garbage: Enforce Standards Automatically (60% Fewer PR Comments)"
- ✅ "3-Week Specs in 2.6 Hours: 4 AI Agents Generate 25 Specifications ($216K/Year Savings)"
- ✅ "Your Copilot Doesn't Know Your Stack: Make It Architecture-Aware (73x Faster Setup)"

### C. Social Proof Integration

**Every claim needs proof:**
- "73x faster" → "Real example: 10,287 lines in 47 minutes vs 3 weeks manual"
- "$216K saved" → "ROI calculator based on $150/hr × 1,460 hours saved"
- "60% fewer PR comments" → "Case study: Engineering team at [Company]"
